Music Credits

Background music for Undiscovered was composed by Daniel Peterschmidt. Daniel worked closely with Annie, Elah, and Christopher to create cues (short songs) that enchanced the narrative, but didn't distract the listener. One piece of feedback that Daniel often got back? Too many notes (Daniel is in no way comparing himself to Mozart). Listen to a selection of the cues made for the show below.

Undiscovered's theme song "The Catch" was composed by i am robot and proud. Annie found the song after Spotify's algorithm suggested it. Shaw-Han Liem, the man behind the project, wrote the song for a composition project while he was in grad school. When Annie discovered it, she asked if Undiscovered could use it and Shaw-Han realized he still had the original session file. The rest is history. You can listen to and buy his music here.
